Day1. ARRIVAL

Welcome to Saudi Arabia, famous for oil, to be the origin of Islam, Arabian horses, the world’s largest sand desert (Rub’ Al Khali), the world’s largest oasis (Al-Ahsa) and much more.

Arrive in Dammam and get transferred to your hotel. Check in to your hotel and freshen up. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure and do a little bit of exploring on your own.

Day2. ITHRA TOUR

Breakfast at the hotel

You will be picked up from your hotel and embark on an exciting tour of King Abdelaziz Center for World Culture also known as Ithra. Arrive at Ithra where you will take a glimpse of the leading cultural and creative destination for talent development and cross-cultural experiences.

Enjoy Lunch at one of the many restaurants at Ithra. Ithra also has many museums you will get the chance to expand your experience by exploring one of them.

The tour has now come to end. You will be dropped off at your hotel where you can relax and unwind for the rest of the day.

Day3. Al Ahsa Oasis Tour

Breakfast at the hotel

Be prepared for a day tour across the historical landmarks of Al Ahsan

Arrive at AlQaisariya market which is an old market located in Al-Ahsa. Handicraft Souq. Proceeding the tour, head now to the handicraft market also known as Craftsmen Market, which features some of the finest heritage products.

Your next stop will be the UNESCO world heritage site. Jabal Al-Qara it’s known for some famous caves in the mountain including Bu Saleh, Al Eid, An Naqa, Al Mua’adhamah, and Al Mahbub

Proceed to a local farm for lunch. Take a tour around the farm before heading back to the hotel.

The tour has now come to end. You will be dropped off at your hotel where you can relax and unwind for the rest of the day.

Day4. Departure:

Breakfast at the hotel

Enjoy some free time depending on your flight time (if applicable) and check out from the hotel. It’s time to say goodbye and have a safe flight home.
