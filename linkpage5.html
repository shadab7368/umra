CLASSIC SAUDI ARABIA – 6 DAYS

Schedule:

Everyday (You can pick the date freely and we will arrange the trip for you based on it)

Tour Highlights

6 Days 5 Nights Riyadh & Dammam.


Day1. Arrival

Welcome to Saudi Arabia, famous for oil, to be the origin of Islam, Arabian horses, the world’s largest sand desert (Rub’ Al Khali), the world’s largest oasis (Al-Ahsa) and much more.

Arrive in Riyadh and get transferred to your hotel. Check in to your hotel and freshen up. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure and do a little bit of exploring on your own.


Overnight stay in Riyadh

Day2. Riyadh Pulse Tour:

Breakfast at the hotel. You will be picked up from your hotel and embark on an exciting tour of Riyadh.

Pass by Al Murabba Historical Palace Marvel at the unique architecture of Al Murabba Palace and take some pictures. Get a glimpse of history at the National Museum, one of the most popular museums located at the King Abdulaziz Historical Centre. Take a look at Al Masmak Fortress, an important landmark of Saudi heritage. Enjoy Lunch at Najd Village restaurant

Enjoy a tour of Al Turaif in Ad Diriyah, the original home of the Saudi royal family. You will be dropped off at your hotel, so enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

Overnight stay in Riyadh

Day3. Safari (Desert Experience)

Breakfast at the hotel. Set out from your hotel for an adventure of a lifetime.

Arrive at thumama or red sands park. Experience the thrill of off-roading with a jeep & Sandboarding

Enjoy a pre-packed lunch meal on Arabian-style seating

Later on transfer to the airport to catch a flight to Dammam. (Domestic flights not included). Arrive in Dammam. Transfer to the hotel.

Overnight stay in Dammam

Day4. Ithra Tour

Breakfast at the hotel. You will be picked up from your hotel and embark on an exciting tour of King Abdelaziz Center for World Culture also known as Ithra.

Arrive at Ithra where you will take a glimpse of the leading cultural and creative destination for talent development and cross-cultural experiences.

Enjoy Lunch at one of the many restaurants at Ithra

Ithra also has many museums you will get the chance to expand your experience by exploring one of them. The tour has now come to end. You will be dropped off at your hotel where you can relax and unwind for the rest of the day.

Meal: Breakfast, Lunch

Overnight stay in Dammam

Day5. AL AHSAA OASIS TOUR:

Breakfast at the hotel. Be prepared for a day tour across the historical landmarks of Al Ahsan. Arrive at AlQaisariya market which is an old market located in Al-Ahsa.

Proceeding the tour, head now to the handicraft market also known as Craftsmen Market, which features some of the finest heritage products. Your next stop will be the UNESCO world heritage site. Jabal Al-Qara it’s known for some famous caves in the mountain including Bu Saleh, Al Eid, An Naqa, Al Mua’adhamah, and Al Mahbub

Proceed to a local farm for lunch, Take a tour around the farm before heading back to the hotel.

The tour has now come to end. You will be dropped off at your hotel where you can relax and unwind for the rest of the day

Overnight stay in Dammam

Day6. Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy some free time depending on your flight time (if applicable) and check out from the hotel. It’s time to say goodbye and have a safe flight home
