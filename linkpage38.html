ADVENTURES ACROSS SAUDI ARABIA â€“ 10 DAYS

The ultimate getaway across Saudi Arabia awaits with this tailor-made 9-night package that will take you through some of the country’s most popular cities. Start this adventure in Riyadh, where you will explore various historical and cultural attractions such as Al Murabba Historical Palace, the National Museum and Edge of the World. Next you will transfer to AlUla, the cultural jewel of Saudi Arabia, and visit iconic landmarks including Hegra, Jabal Ikmah and AlUla Old Town. The third stop on this one-of-a-kind package is to the NEOM area of the Tabuk region and its stunning attractions such as Mugha’ir Shu’ayb, Tayeb Al-Ism and the 12 natural springs in Maqna. The last stop on this trip will be Jeddah and during the visit, you will get to enjoy a Red Sea tour as well as a tour of the Al Balad, the historical area of the city. Book your package now and get ready to explore Saudi Arabia like never before!

Day1: Arrival

Arrive in Riyadh and get your hotel check-in sorted (if applicable). Based on your arrival time, you can spend the rest of the day at leisure.

Day2: Riyadh plus tour

Start the day with a tour that includes iconic attractions such as Al Murabba Historical Palace, the National Museum and Al Masmak Fortress. Once done, you will head to Takya, where you can relish some amazing fusion delicacies before visiting Al Bujairi Heritage Park and Ad Diriyah, the original home of the Saudi royal family.

Day3: Explore Riyadh & transfer to AlUla

Get ready for an adventure that starts with a thrilling off-roading experience as you make way to Edge of the World, one of the most popular tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia. Once you arrive, you will enjoy the views of the never-ending horizon and bask in the natural beauty of your surroundings. The tour also includes indulging in a pre-packed meal in a traditional Arabian setting before heading to the airport for your flight to AlUla.

Day4: Hegra tour

Start your tour with a trip to Madain Saleh (Hegra), followed by a visit to the popular Ashar Valley. Once done, you will head out to explore AlUla Old Town and the famous Elephant Rock.

Day5: AlUla city tour & transfer to Tabuk

Head out for a fun trip to AlUla’s famous Jabal Ikmah. After that, you will get to explore Dadan and visit the ‘Lion Tombs’, which was once the burial site of the elite. Driven to Tabuk for your next hotel check-in (if applicable).

Day6: Trip to NEOM area

Embark on a tour of historical sites & natural beauty in the NEOM area of the Tabuk region. During the trip, you will visit popular attractions including Mugha’ir Shu’ayb, Tayeb Al-Ism and the 12 natural springs in Maqna. The trip will also include seafood lunch at a local restaurant before heading back to the hotel.

Day7: Deesa Great Valley trip & transfer to Jeddah

Bask in the natural beauty of Deesa Great Valley (Wadi Al Disah) and admire the stunning views of mountainous terrain and flowing springs. During the trip, you will also get to indulge in a delicious meal at one of the area’s local farms before heading to the airport for your flight to Jeddah.

Day8: Red Sea tour

On this thrilling boat ride, you enjoy a fun day under the sun and partake in fun water activities such as snorkeling. You will also get to indulge in a delicious meal with terrific sea views.

Day9: Al Balad trip & transfer to Riyadh

Look forward to visiting popular attractions such as Nasseef House and Baghdadi House on a tour that will help you discover Jeddah’s ancient heritage. You will also explore the gold market in Gabel Street before heading to the Al-Qawsiya historical springs (Aeen Faraj Yuser) and Shafei and Ukash mosques for a short tour. Once the tour is over, you will head to the airport for your flight back to Riyadh.

Day10: Departure

Check out from your hotel (if applicable). Depending on your pick-up time, you can explore the city on foot or head to the airport for your flight (if applicable).
