Tour Highlights:

6 Days 5 Nights Jeddah & Al Ula.

Day1. Arrival

Welcome to Saudi Arabia, famous for oil, to be the origin of Islam, Arabian horses, the world’s largest sand desert (Rub’ Al Khali), the world’s largest oasis (Al-Ahsa) and much more.

Arrive in Jeddah and get transferred to your hotel. Check in to your hotel and freshen up. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure and do a little bit of exploring on your own.

Overnight stay in Jeddah

Day2. RED SEA

Breakfast at the hotel, Get picked up from your hotel for an exciting tour.

Take a boat in the Red Sea and enjoy snorkeling, diving, and water sports. 

Enjoy the beautiful view of the sea while being served some snacks

So now we head back to the land to fuel up.

Enjoy Lunch at Twayna Restaurant.

You will be dropped off at your hotel, so enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

Overnight stay in Jeddah

Day3. Historical Jeddah

Breakfast at the hotel, Get picked up from your hotel for an exciting tour of Jeddah’s historical downtown.

Get a chance to immerse yourself and appreciate the architecture of old houses such as Nasseef House and Baghdadi House.

A trip to old houses & Al Ma’amar Mosque. Visit Jamjoom House, Noor Wali House and Matbouli House, along with one of the oldest mosques in Jeddah, Al Ma’amar Mosque.

Take a trip around the gold market in Gabel Street and then visit the Aeen Faraj Yuser, Shafei and Ukash mosques.

Enjoy traditional lunch in local Naranj restaurant..

Take lots of photos of the iconic Jeddah Fountain, the highest in the world.

Head to the airport for your flight to AlUla. (Internal flights are not included)

Transfer to your hotel in AlUla and get some much needed rest.

Overnight stay in AlUla 

Day4. Hegra Tour:

Breakfast at the hotel. Get picked up from your hotel for an exciting excursion around AlUla.

Marvel at Saudi Arabia’s first UNESCO World Heritage Site, Madain Saleh, also known as Hegra.

Enjoy Lunch at Heritage Garden Restaurant.

Explore AlUla Old Town and immerse yourself in the fascinating history of this ancient city made up of nearly 900 houses, 400 shops and five town squares.

Feast your eyes upon the Elephant Rock, a giant sandstone monolith that resembles an elephant with its trunk touching the ground.

You will be dropped off at your hotel, so enjoy a relaxing evening at your hotel.

Overnight stay in AlUla.

Day5. Open Air Museum

Breakfast at the hotel. Get picked up from your hotel to get some insight into AlUla’s history.

Explore the red-tinted steep mountain, where visitors ca n admire ancient writings and inscriptions that date back to the Lihyanite civilization. Discover the magnificent history of Dadan and visit the ‘Lion Tombs’, the burial site of the elite marked by seated lion sculptures.

Visit a farm in AlUla and enjoy a delicious lunch while you’re there.

You will be dropped off at your hotel.

Overnight stay in AlUla

Day6. Departure

Breakfast at the hotel, Enjoy some free time depending on your flight time (if applicable) and check out from the hotel. It’s time to say goodbye and have a safe flight home.
